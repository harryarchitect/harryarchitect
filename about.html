About Us

Harry Architect was established in Pattoki Punjab, Pakistan in 2022. The company grew organically and gained extensive experience in Pakistan. Currently providing services all over Pakistan, Harry Architect has accumulated an impressive portfolio of projects that spans the breadth of building genres – from residential, commercial, leisure, retail, and interior design. The years of experience and absolute commitment to quality enhance the reputation that Harry Architect has as a premier contractor.

Our separate construction and design departments allow us the flexibility of providing a complete turnkey service. Also within the Harry Architect group is an independent bespoke joinery, producing high-end designs for both the local and international market using the finest woods and veneers from renewable sources.
