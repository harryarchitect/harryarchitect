Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your personal and project information.

Introduction

Welcome to Harry Architect. We are highly committed to respecting and protecting the privacy of our visitors and clients. This Privacy Policy page explains how we collect, use, and safe-keep your personal and project details when you interact with our estimation forms, contact cards, or consultation setups.

Information We Collect

When you submit a design inquiry or estimation request on our home screen or contact portals, we collect essential project details to serve you better. This includes:

  • Your personal identification (Full Name, Active WhatsApp or Mobile Phone Number).
  • Detailed technical features (Plot length/width size, society location, preferred city name).
  • Specific custom data (Interior requirements, selected building genres, architectural layout choices).

How We Use Your Data

Harry Architect strictly utilizes your shared data for targeted business optimization. We never trade, distribute, or leak your private information. Your records are used to:

  • Draft highly tailored computerized maps, 3D front elevations, or cost analysis breakdowns.
  • Communicate project updates and provide custom quote updates via WhatsApp or mobile network.
  • Analyze internal metrics to enhance our digital user interfaces and client communication workflows.

Data Security & Integrity

Our separate design, planning, and structural departments implement proper security layers to ensure your blueprints, contact numbers, and payment receipts are highly confidential. Authorized senior representatives alone handle critical client profiles during site evaluation phases.

Policy Updates & Contact

We reserve the right to alter or upgrade this policy layout to stay fully synced with Pakistan's digital privacy laws. If you have any serious concerns, complaints, or feedback regarding your data safety, please write to our executive staff directly via Harry846376@gmail.com.
